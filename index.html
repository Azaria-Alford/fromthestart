Do Moths migrate?

Yes, Moths do migrate

Moths do migrate but not all of them, only certain species like for example The Hummingbird Hawk Moths and The Green Page Moths.


The Hummingbird Hawk Moth

The Hummingbird Moth can be found year round in Southern Europe and Asia but it's migration range can extends into Northern African in the Winter. During the summer, it pushes northward into Europe and Russia.
